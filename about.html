25 Years of Innovation

Founded by Deepak Mozarkar, D'Soft was born from a simple observation: students were being taught "how to code" but never "how to think."

D'Soft was founded with a single vision: to bridge the gap between academic theory and industry reality. Over the last quarter of a century, we have pioneered training methodologies that focus on logic building rather than rote memorization.
