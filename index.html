Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the "Mirror Coder" trap?

Mirror coding is when you can follow a tutorial or copy code, but feel lost when starting from a blank screen. At D'Soft, we focus on "Logic-First" training, ensuring you understand the 'Why' behind every line so you can build anything independently.

Does D'Soft provide 100% Job Assistance?

Yes. Through our industrial wing, ARDSOFT IT SOLUTIONS, we provide 100% placement assistance. We don't just teach; we prepare you for the industry with live projects and internal internship opportunities.

Is this course suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. Most of our students start from zero. Our teaching methodology is designed to build your foundation carefully, whether you are a first-year college student or looking to switch careers into tech.

Are the classes offline or online?

We offer a Hybrid Model to give you the best of both worlds. You get intense personal mentorship and hands-on practice at our Nagpur campus, combined with flexible online support, recorded sessions, and digital resources for continuous learning anywhere.
